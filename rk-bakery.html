Our Story

A Family Tradition
Since 2010

RK Bakery started as a small home kitchen in Madurai, born from a grandmother's treasured recipes and a family's passion for baking. Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across the city.

Every cake, every sweet, every bite carries the warmth of homemade goodness. We never compromise on quality — using fresh dairy, real fruits, and zero artificial flavors.
